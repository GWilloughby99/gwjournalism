Darts progress dashboard

Regular updates tracking my performance in each match including average, first-nine average and big score trackers

Welcome to my darts dashboard which is regularly updating each time I log a game. I took up playing darts seriously during the start of the Covid-19 pandemic because well, there wasn't really much else to do.

I began watching some videos (thank you Wayne Mardle) and quickly found out I needed a lot of practice. As a former cricketer, I know full well how important rhythm is. I hoped this would bode well for me as I tried to step up my game. I set the difficulty level relatively high and started throwing for hours each day.

Work has made it hard for me to play as regularly as I did but I've maintained my motivation and dedicated time to getting better. Below is a series of charts that show my progress.

Most of the matches are first to six legs with the computer averaging between 60-70. The more recent games have been first to 10 with a few matches involving set play.
